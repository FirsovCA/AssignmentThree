Why Bitter Chocolate is Good for You

  • Lowering Blood Pressure: Dark chocolate helps lower blood pressure, especially when consumed with cocoa without sugar.
  • Prevention of Cardiovascular Diseases: Dark chocolate prevents inflammatory processes that can lead to cardiovascular diseases.
  • Improved Blood Circulation: The flavonoids in dark chocolate promote better blood flow to the brain.
  • Stabilization of Cholesterol Levels: Consuming dark chocolate helps stabilize bad cholesterol (LDL) levels.
  • Improved Insulin Sensitivity: Dark chocolate helps improve insulin sensitivity, reducing the risk of developing diabetes.
  • Stress Reduction: Consuming dark chocolate lowers cortisol levels (the stress hormone) in the body.
  • Antioxidant Properties: Dark chocolate is rich in antioxidants, which protect the body’s cells from oxidative stress and slow down aging.
  • Prevention of Inflammation: Regular consumption of dark chocolate reduces levels of C-reactive protein in the blood, which decreases inflammation.
  • Benefits for Obese Adults: Cocoa in dark chocolate helps normalize blood pressure in people suffering from obesity.
